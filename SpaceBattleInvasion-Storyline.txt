Quick Trivia: Bwindi Impenetrable Forest of Uganda > this is a forest that derived its name from the thick forests of tall bamboo and dense ground cover of ferns, vines and other plants of the Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. It is difficult to enter on foot. Bwindi is known to be "IMPENETRABLE" in the Runyakitara language. It is hard to access  hence the name Bwindi meaning Impenetrable, Impenetrable Forest! It is home to the planets endangered mountain gorillas.
